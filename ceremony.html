Travel

The Inn at Millrace Pond is located in Hope, NJ, approximately 60 miles west of New York City and 10 minutes east of the Delaware Water Gap.

From East, West, and North

Exit 12 off Interstate 80, then south on Route 521 less than one mile to blinking light in Hope. Left on Route 519 two blocks.

From South

Exit 3 off Interstate 78, then one mile west on Route 22. Right on Route 519 approx 18 miles to blinking light in Hope. Right on Route 519 two blocks.

Nearest Major Airport

Newark International, NJ (EWR) 48 miles

Interstate 78 west to Route 24 west to Interstate 287 north to Interstate 80 west. Exit 12 off Interstate 80, then south on Route 521 less than one mile to blinking light in Hope. Left on Route 519 two blocks.
