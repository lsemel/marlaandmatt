Maid of Honor

Jenn Eisenberg

If it weren’t for Jenn, the chances that Marla and Matt would have met would be highly improbable. Marla has been great friends with Jenn since freshman year of high school, but their paths were meant to cross even before that when Jenn’s mom fortuitously saw a picture of Marla on Marla’s mom’s desk years before and noted the striking similarity between the two awkward looking preteens. Jenn also happens to be Matt’s first cousin. She can usually be seen biking the streets of New York City, bringing dance education into public schools, or contorting her body in ways that don’t seem humanly possible on a yoga mat.

Bridesmaids

Kristen Lombardo

Marla and Kristen have been close friends since they were both nine years old and grew up on the same street. Over time, their friendship became stronger throughout high school and NYU (which they both attended and were roommates senior year). Kristen has been an amazing friend to Marla over the years and they have been through many of life’s fortunes and misfortunes together. At any given time Kristen can be found somewhere in the United States travelling for her job in television production, drinking a healthy glass of wine, or keeping up to date on the latest celebrity gossip.

Christina Bach

During medical school orientation week, Marla found Christina heading to the beach and decided to join her. It’s a good thing she did because she ended up becoming one of Marla’s closest friends, and Marla isn’t sure how she could have survived Grenada without her. Over four years of med school they continued to stay close and enjoyed living in NYC for rotations where they do a lot of eating and shopping. Marla is so excited and honored to be part of Christina’s wedding in May. Christina likes pandas, binge watching Law & Order, and cupcakes. She’ll be starting her residency in Internal Medicine in Buffalo in the summer!

Best Man

Lee Semel

Lee is Matt’s older brother of five and a half years and is one of the strongest influences in Matt’s life. When the family got a video camera in the mid 80’s, Lee started making videos, which Matt emulated, and eventually ended up being his career choice. Lee is a talented artist, and photographer. Lee can be found taking photos of unsuspecting subjects on the streets of NYC, so watch out!

Groomsmen

Shawn Koonin

Matt and Shawn have been friends since third grade, when Shawn’s family moved to NJ from South Africa, via Toronto. Shawn and Matt share a love of games and knowing random Simpsons quotes. Shawn can be seen on the streets of Brooklyn looking impeccably dressed or recording his music.

Doug Letterman

Doug and Matt became friends sophomore year of college, and quickly realized that they had many of the same interests. Doug helped introduce Matt to camping and hiking, and the two of them have traveled cross-country, to Nepal, Patagonia, and countless trips to the Adirondacks. Matt frequently finds himself in life-threatening situations with Doug Letterman. Doug enjoys ultra marathons, tandem bike riding with his wife, and reading about catastrophic technological failures.

Miguel Drake-McLaughlin

Early on at NYU, Matt and Miguel met, and have been friends ever since. They have worked together on countless film projects for the past 17 years, been roommates for five years, and are now business partners at Cowboy Bear Ninja. Miguel is an extremely talented cinematographer. Usually found in an epic wool sweater, Miguel can be seen rock climbing, cooking up Michelin star-worthy meals, or cycling the streets of Brooklyn.
